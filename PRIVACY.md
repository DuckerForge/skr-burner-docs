# Privacy Policy for SKR Burner

SKR Burner does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

- Your wallet address is used solely for on-chain transactions (burning SKR tokens on Solana).
- All activity is public on the Solana blockchain.
- No tracking, analytics, cookies, or third-party services are used.
- No personal information is requested or stored.

Burning tokens is irreversible. Use at your own risk.
